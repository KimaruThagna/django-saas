KEEN EYE is a web based medical imaging processor that runs on top of a deep learning model. This facilitates the concept of tele radiology by allowing clinicians and medical practitioners to upload medical imaging scans which are submitted to a state of the art deep learning model/Algorithim which predicts, with high accuracy, the onset or presence of an abonormality in the images. Keen eye aims to alleviate the current pressure on radiologists to deliver stellar results despite their shortage and high demand. With this platform, the through put is set to be higher and thus, serve a larger populace.
